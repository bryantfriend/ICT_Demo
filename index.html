Presentations are like stories. The ORDER of the slides matters!

The story is out of order! Clean -> Muddy -> Bath? The audience is confused!

The Hamburger Rule 🍔

A great presentation is like a delicious hamburger. It has three important parts that must go in order!

🍞

1. The Beginning

The Top Bun. The Title Slide and the Introduction. Tell them what you are going to talk about!

🥬

2. The Middle

The Meat & Veggies. The main details, steps, or story in a logical order.

🍞

3. The End

The Bottom Bun. The Conclusion or "Thank You" slide. Wrap it up neatly!
